GearGo

Student Equipment Rental Platform

A peer-to-peer rental platform within campuses — letting students rent tools, instruments, and study gear from each other. Less waste. More community. Zero bureaucracy.

The Problem

💸
Students overspend on single-use gear

A lab project needs a soldering iron. A photography assignment needs a tripod. A music module needs a keyboard. Students buy these, spend ₹2,000–₹8,000 each time, use them for two weeks, and then let them gather dust under their beds indefinitely.

🗄️
Hostels are full of idle equipment

Walk through any engineering hostel and you'll find a graveyard of bought-once items — Raspberry Pi kits, drawing boards, guitar capos, camera lenses, coding keyboards. These things exist. They're just not connected to the people who currently need them.

🤷
No trusted way to lend or borrow

Word-of-mouth borrowing happens informally, but without accountability. Items don't come back. Friendships get strained. There's no record of who has what, no deposit system, and no way to surface what's actually available on your campus right now.

"Everything on campus is owned by someone
who doesn't need it right now."

How It Works

Step 01 — List
Post what you own

Got a tripod you used twice? List it on GearGo with photos, condition rating, and your daily/weekly rental price. Takes under 2 minutes. Your item is now visible to the whole campus.

Step 02 — Discover
Browse what's available

Search by category, keyword, or department. See who owns it, their rating, and pick-up availability. Everything is filtered to your campus so you're never more than a walk away.

Step 03 — Rent
Book and collect

Send a rental request with your dates. The owner confirms. A refundable security deposit is held digitally. You collect the item, use it, return it — and both parties rate each other.

Step 04 — Earn
Your gear pays back

Owners earn rental income on items they weren't using anyway. A student with a good camera kit can recover their purchase cost within a semester. Idle assets become passive income.

Trust & Safety

Verified Identity

All users sign up with their college email. Only enrolled students of that institution can access the platform — no outsiders.

Digital Deposit

A refundable security deposit is held in the platform wallet during the rental period. Released automatically on verified return.

Peer Ratings

Both renter and owner rate each transaction. A visible trust score on every profile means accountability without enforcement overhead.
