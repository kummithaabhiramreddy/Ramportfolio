BytesLoop⌄
Unused Data Converted to Credits

Every day, millions of gigabytes of mobile data expire unused. BytesLoop tracks leftover data on your phone, converts it to credits before it vanishes, and lets you spend or save those credits after your plan period resets.

"You paid for that data. It shouldn't just disappear at midnight."

Who I am

Building for the people
tech usually skips.

I'm Kummitha Abhiram Reddy — an IT student at SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram. I come from Thimmareddypalem village in Nellore district, and that background shapes everything I build.

I don't build because it's trendy. I build because I've seen the problems firsthand — villages without fast emergency response, skilled workers with no digital presence, students throwing money at equipment they'll use once, and ordinary people losing mobile data they already paid for.

My work sits at the intersection of product thinking and practical engineering — ideas that come from real observation, built with real code.

Achievements

UDBHAV 2K26 — National Level Hackathon

UDBHAV 2K26 was a hackathon introduced exclusively for 1st-year students at SRKR Engineering College — the first time an event like this was held at the college. I competed under the team name HackHorizon, and this was also my first time participating in a hackathon of this scale.

The event was organized by the Department of Information Technology along with the Computer Society of India (CSI) — SRKREC Student Chapter, held on 5–6 March 2026. It covered AI&DS, CSBS and IT teams, and was a great first exposure to building under pressure with a team.
