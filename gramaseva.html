Gramaseva

Rural & Urban Worker Connect

India's farmers, plumbers, electricians, and masons are invisible online. Gramaseva gives skilled workers from rural and urban areas a platform to reach clients — bringing discoverability and dignity to the informal workforce.

Seasonal workers, crop harvesters, irrigation workers — skilled people with no way to reach farmers who need them across district boundaries.

🔧

🧱

The Problem

For Workers — no visibility

All work comes through personal connections. When those dry up, there's nothing. No profile, no portfolio, no reviews.

They cannot charge a fair market rate because clients have no way to compare quality. Undercutting is the only strategy.

Skilled workers with 20 years of experience look identical on paper to a first-day apprentice — because neither has any paper.

Seasonal gaps in work mean income dries up. There's no way to find work outside their village radius during the off-season.

For Clients — no trust

Finding skilled help in a new area means asking neighbors, calling relatives, or taking a risk on someone unknown. No reviews. No history.

Price negotiation is awkward and opaque. There's no baseline, so both sides guess — and distrust follows.

No accountability after the job. If work is substandard, there's no recourse. No rating to warn others. The same bad actor keeps finding new victims.

Urban clients who relocate to semi-rural areas have no idea who the reliable local workers are. Starting from zero every time.

How Gramaseva Works

1
Create a Profile
A page to your name

Workers create a profile in Telugu or English — their trade, years of experience, area of operation, daily rate, and photos of past work. A QR code version means even workers without smartphones can have a shareable identity card.

2
Discover Workers
Search by trade & location

Clients search Gramaseva by work type, pincode, or distance. Workers nearby show up with their profile, rating, availability, and rate. Direct messaging opens from the profile — no intermediary.

3
Confirm & Work
Agree on terms, then begin

Both parties confirm the job scope, date, and rate through the platform. An optional digital agreement is generated. The worker gets a timestamped booking confirmation — proof of work they can show anywhere.

4
Review & Build Reputation
Reviews that compound over time

After each job, client and worker review each other. A mason with 50 five-star reviews becomes the most trusted mason in their district — no middleman required. Good work speaks for itself, permanently.
